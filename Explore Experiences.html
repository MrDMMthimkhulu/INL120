Explore Experiences with Vacation Oasis

Unforgettable journeys into the beauty of South Africa

Wildlife Safaris

Location: Kruger National Park, Mpumalanga

Step into the wild with our tailor-made safari experiences at Kruger National Park. Traverse breathtaking landscapes, spotting the majestic Big Five and other remarkable species in their natural habitat. Whether you choose a guided game drive or a thrilling walking safari, each moment creates lasting memories.

Location: Addo Elephant National Park, Eastern Cape

Encounter one of the largest elephant populations in the world at Addo Elephant National Park. Drive through scenic landscapes, watching elephants play and soaking in the beauty of the Eastern Cape. With our expert guides, every moment becomes unforgettable.

Location: Hluhluwe-iMfolozi Park, KwaZulu-Natal

Join us at Hluhluwe-iMfolozi Park, where you can see the Big Five while learning about conservation efforts. Our knowledgeable guides share stories of the wildlife and the region’s cultural heritage, connecting you to the ongoing conservation journey.

Adventure Activities

Location: Pilanesberg National Park, North West

Pilanesberg National Park is perfect for thrill-seekers! Enjoy hot air balloon rides and zip-lining above stunning landscapes. Experience the awakening wildlife below while floating over the park at sunrise.

Location: Drakensberg Mountains, KwaZulu-Natal

Discover the awe-inspiring Drakensberg Mountains with rock climbing, abseiling, and mountain biking amidst stunning scenery. Join guided hikes to hidden waterfalls and ancient rock art sites for a blend of excitement and cultural immersion.

Location: Tsitsikamma National Park, Eastern Cape

Get your adrenaline pumping at Tsitsikamma National Park. From bungee jumping to hiking the famous Otter Trail, this destination is a haven for adventure lovers. We guide you through diverse ecosystems while sharing local stories.

Historical Sites

Location: Robben Island, Western Cape

Immerse yourself in South Africa’s profound history at Robben Island, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Walk in the footsteps of Nelson Mandela and hear powerful stories of resilience from former political prisoners.

Location: The Apartheid Museum, Gauteng

Explore South Africa's past at The Apartheid Museum. This moving experience reveals the nation's journey toward democracy through thought-provoking exhibits and personal stories.

Location: Mapungubwe National Park, Limpopo

Step back in time at Mapungubwe National Park, where ancient civilizations thrived. Our expert guides share stories of the region's rich heritage while you enjoy breathtaking views of the Limpopo River.

Hiking Trails

Location: Table Mountain National Park, Western Cape

Join us at Table Mountain National Park, where iconic trails await. Ascend for breathtaking views of Cape Town and the Atlantic Ocean, with paths for all fitness levels.

Location: Blyde River Canyon, Mpumalanga

Hike through Blyde River Canyon, one of the largest canyons on Earth. Our guides reveal the secrets of stunning geological wonders along the way.

Location: Drakensberg Mountains, KwaZulu-Natal

Discover the rugged beauty of the Drakensberg Mountains with hikes through stunning scenery. Our expert guides share ancient rock art and majestic waterfalls along the way.

Wine Tasting

Location: Cape Winelands, Western Cape

Indulge in the Cape Winelands, where lush vineyards and award-winning wineries await. Join guided tours for tastings and food pairings, soaking in stunning mountain views.

Location: Constantia Valley, Western Cape

Explore Constantia Valley, renowned for its rich history and exceptional wines. Our tours reveal the art of winemaking while enjoying exquisite tastings.

Location: Robertson Wine Valley, Western Cape

Escape to the Robertson Wine Valley, where charming wine estates welcome you with open arms. Sample local wines, cheeses, and delicacies while enjoying the picturesque surroundings. With its friendly atmosphere and beautiful scenery, this hidden gem is perfect for those seeking a relaxed wine-tasting experience. Vacation Oasis will help you uncover the best of Robertson, creating a wine lover’s paradise!

Beaches and Islands

Location: Durban Beaches, KwaZulu-Natal

Soak up the sun on the stunning beaches of Durban, where warm waters and golden sands beckon. The Golden Mile is a lively stretch perfect for swimming, surfing, and soaking in the vibrant atmosphere. With Vacation Oasis, you’ll enjoy the best beach experiences, from thrilling water sports to leisurely beach strolls. It’s the ultimate coastal getaway!

Location: : Cape Peninsula, Western Cape

Experience the beauty of the Cape Peninsula, where breathtaking beaches like Camps Bay and Boulders Beach await. Relax on pristine sands, explore charming coastal towns, and spot adorable African penguins at Boulders Beach. With Vacation Oasis, you’ll discover the magic of the coastline while enjoying unique beach experiences that will create lasting memories.

Location: Knysna, Western Cape

Discover the coastal charm of Knysna, located along the Garden Route. This stunning area offers beautiful beaches and lagoons, perfect for water sports, boating, and relaxation. Enjoy the culinary delights of the region during the famous oyster festival while surrounded by picturesque scenery. With Vacation Oasis, we’ll guide you to the best spots for an unforgettable beach vacation!
